Top 10 Companies in the Bay Area for Wage Complaints

Workers can file claims for unpaid wages with the department of labor. According to court and labor department rulings, more than 1,300 Bay Area companies failed to pay workers nearly $20 million in wages in 2014.

These are the top 10 companies in the Bay Area ranked by the amount that courts have compelled them to repay, as well as the number of successful complaints against them.

Notes

We aggregated only the number of successful complaints. We did not count unsuccessful wage claims that did not result in a cash amount awarded..

Source: California Department of Labor
